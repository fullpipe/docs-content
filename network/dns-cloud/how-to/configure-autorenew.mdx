Autorenew is a feature that allows you to automatically renew the validity period of a domain name registered with Scaleway Domains & DNS on a yearly basis.

<Message type="requirement">

- You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
- You have a domain name registered with Scaleway Domains & DNS

</Message>

## How to activate autorenew

1. Click **Domains & DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. 

The Domain & DNS dashboard displays with a list of your internal domains.

2. Click the domain name you want to configure.

3. Click **Activate** under **Autorenew** in the domain information section.

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-domain-autorenew.webp" alt="" />

You have activated autorenew for your domain name.

## How to deactivate autorenew

1. Click **Domains & DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. 

The Domain & DNS dashboard displays with a list of your internal domains.

2. Click the domain name you want to configure.

3. Click **Deactivate** under **Autorenew** in the domain information section.

4. Click **Validate** in the pop-up to confirm. 

You have deactivated autorenew for your domain name.
