DNSSEC uses digital signatures based on public key cryptography to strengthen DNS authentication. You can enable or disable DNSSEC your domain names registered with Scaleway Domains & DNS.

<Message type="requirement">

- You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
- You have a domain name registered with Scaleway Domains & DNS

</Message>

## How to activate DNSSEC

1. Click **Domains and DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. 

 The Domain & DNS dashboard displays. 

2. Click the domain name you want to configure.

3. Click **Activate** next to **DNSSEC** in the domain information section.

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-domain-dnssec.webp" alt="" />

    The key pair generates and DNS for your domain will be enabled.

## How to deactivate DNSSEC

1. Click **Domains and DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. 

  The Domain & DNS dashboard displays.
  
2. Click the domain name you want to configure.

3. Click **Deactivate** next to **DNSSEC** in the domain information section.
